### [★★★★☆] LiteSpeed cPanel Plugin CVE-2026-48172（CVSS 10.0）──最大深刻度の特権昇格脆弱性
**ソース:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/) / [CISA](https://www.cisa.gov/known-exploited-vulnerabilities-catalog)
