### [★★★★★] Aflacジャパン データ侵害 — 438万人の個人情報が流出、23万人は口座情報も
**ソース:** [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/aflac-japan-data-breach-impacts-4-38-million/) / [The Japan Times](https://www.japantimes.co.jp/business/2026/06/30/aflac-hack-4-million/)

### [★★★★★] Azure CLI 標的パスワードスプレー攻撃 — 8100万件超の試行で64組織・78アカウントを侵害
**ソース:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/07/azure-cli-password-spray-hits-at-least.html) / [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/massive-password-spray-campaign-targeting-azure-cli/)
