**ソース:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/06/miasma-worm-hits-73-microsoft-github.html) / [Microsoft Security Blog](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/blog/2026/06/02/preinstall-persistence-inside-red-hat-npm-miasma-credential-stealing-campaign/)
